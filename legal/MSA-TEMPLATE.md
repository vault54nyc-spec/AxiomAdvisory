## 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

1.1 Axiom agrees to perform the professional services described in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") executed by both Parties and incorporated herein by reference. Each SOW is subject to the terms of this Agreement.

1.2 This Agreement governs all current and future SOWs between the Parties unless otherwise expressly agreed in writing.

1.3 Any services, deliverables, or work product not expressly described in an executed SOW are outside the scope of this Agreement and will require a separate written amendment or new SOW.

## 2. FEES, INVOICING, AND PAYMENT TERMS

2.1 **Fees.** Client agrees to pay Axiom the fees set forth in each applicable SOW. All fees are stated in U.S. Dollars.

2.2 **Initiation Deposit.** Where specified in the SOW, an initiation deposit is due upon execution of this Agreement and the applicable SOW. Work will not commence until the deposit is received and cleared.

2.3 **Invoicing.** Axiom will issue invoices to Client at the milestones or intervals specified in the applicable SOW. Invoices will be delivered to the Client contact on file via email.

2.4 **Payment Due Date.** All invoices are due and payable within **fifteen (15) calendar days** of the invoice date ("Due Date"), unless otherwise specified in the applicable SOW.

2.5 **Late Payment Fee.** If Client fails to remit full payment by the Due Date, a late payment fee will accrue on the outstanding balance at the rate of **1.5% per month (18% per annum)**, or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is less. Late fees begin accruing on the first calendar day following the Due Date and continue to accrue until the balance, including all accrued late fees, is paid in full.

2.6 **Work Suspension.** If any invoice remains unpaid past its Due Date, Axiom reserves the right to immediately suspend all work on any active SOW without further notice to Client. Suspension is not a waiver of any rights or remedies available to Axiom under this Agreement or applicable law.

2.7 **Timeline Adjustment.** In the event of work suspension under Section 2.6, all project timelines, milestones, deliverable dates, and go-live targets set forth in the applicable SOW will automatically shift by a period equal to the number of calendar days the work was suspended, plus any additional ramp-up time reasonably required by Axiom upon resumption. The revised timeline will be communicated to Client in writing upon receipt of full payment, including any accrued late fees. Axiom bears no liability for delays resulting from Client's failure to pay.

2.8 **Disputed Invoices.** If Client disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, Client must notify Axiom in writing within five (5) calendar days of the invoice date, specifying the disputed amount and the basis for the dispute. Undisputed amounts remain due and payable by the Due Date.

2.9 **Collection Costs.** If Axiom is required to engage legal counsel or a collection agency to recover overdue amounts, Client agrees to reimburse Axiom for all reasonable attorneys' fees, court costs, and collection costs incurred.

## 3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

3.1 **Axiom Ownership During Engagement.** All work product, deliverables, designs, code, content, brand assets, documentation, and any other materials created by Axiom in connection with services performed under this Agreement ("Work Product") remain the sole and exclusive property of Axiom Executive Advisory LLC until final payment in full has been received by Axiom.

3.2 **Transfer Upon Final Payment.** Upon receipt of full and final payment of all fees, expenses, and any accrued charges owed under all applicable SOWs, Axiom will execute a written assignment transferring all right, title, and interest in the Work Product to Client, including all applicable intellectual property rights. Until such assignment is executed, Client has no ownership claim over any Work Product.

3.3 **License During Engagement.** Pending final payment, Axiom grants Client a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use any delivered Work Product solely for Client's internal review and feedback purposes. This license does not include the right to publish, distribute, or commercially exploit any Work Product.

3.4 **Axiom Tools and Pre-Existing IP.** This Agreement does not transfer any rights in Axiom's proprietary tools, frameworks, methodologies, processes, or pre-existing intellectual property. Where Axiom incorporates such materials into Work Product, Axiom grants Client a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use those elements solely as incorporated in the delivered Work Product.

3.5 **Third-Party Materials.** Where Work Product incorporates third-party licensed materials, fonts, stock assets, or open-source software, Client acknowledges that such materials are subject to the applicable third-party license terms. Axiom will identify material third-party components in the applicable SOW.

4.1 Each Party agrees to hold in strict confidence all non-public information of the other Party that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information ("Confidential Information").

4.2 Neither Party will disclose the other Party's Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent, except to employees, contractors, or advisors who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations no less protective than those herein.

4.3 Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving Party; (b) was rightfully known to the receiving Party prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed by the receiving Party without use of Confidential Information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the receiving Party gives prompt written notice to the disclosing Party.

4.4 This Section survives termination or expiration of the Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

## 8. TERM AND TERMINATION

8.1 **Term.** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until all active SOWs are completed or the Agreement is otherwise terminated.

8.2 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within ten (10) calendar days of receiving written notice of the breach.

8.3 **Termination for Non-Payment.** Axiom may terminate this Agreement and any active SOW upon written notice if Client fails to remit any invoice payment within thirty (30) calendar days of the Due Date.

8.4 **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (a) Client will promptly pay all outstanding fees for services rendered through the date of termination; (b) all Work Product remains the property of Axiom unless and until all outstanding amounts are paid in full; and (c) each Party will return or destroy the other Party's Confidential Information upon request.

8.5 Sections 3, 4, 7, 8.4, and 9 survive termination or expiration of this Agreement.
